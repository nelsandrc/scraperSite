Ongoing Cases

These graphs are updated weekly to show the ongoing spread of COVID. While vaccinations are rolling out at an increasing rate, we're not out of the woods yet and we hope these figures will serve as a reminder to stay safe and practice the correct safety precautions. Be aware that the state of Hawaii does not report case numbers on the weekend.

Economic Impact

Beyond the human impact of the pandemic, COVID-19 has clearly impacted the economy and its effects will be felt for years to come. Years of recovery from the 2008 recession have been undone and the country faces higher unemployement than it has in half a decade.
